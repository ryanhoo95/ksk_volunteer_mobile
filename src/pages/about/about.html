About Us Mission

(1) To provide food to the marginalized community.

(2) To provide basic medical care to the marginalized community.

(3) To help the marginalized community to re-enter society by providing counseling and other relevant assistance.

 Vision

To support and complement government efforts to promote a caring Malaysia in which every individual has his/her most basic needs fulfilled and human dignity and worth respected.
